Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a comforting and hearty Italian-American baked pasta dish made with layers of wide, flat lasagna noodles, a rich meat sauce (often with tomatoes), creamy béchamel sauce, and generous amounts of cheese, typically mozzarella and Parmesan. It's baked until bubbly and golden brown.

Ingredients

  • tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Brown ground meat, add onion and garlic, then stir in tomatoes and seasonings; simmer.
  2. Melt butter, whisk in flour, gradually add milk, cook until thick, then season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  3. Combine ricotta, egg, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Spread meat sauce in a baking dish, then layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, béchamel, mozzarella, and Parmesan; repeat.
  5. Cover and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake 15-20 minutes more until bubbly and golden.
  6. Let lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving.
